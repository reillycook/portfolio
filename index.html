About Me

Reilly Cook

Journalist & Video Storyteller based in Chicago

I am a passionate multimedia journalist specializing in video storytelling and investigative reporting. Currently pursuing my Master of Science in Journalism with a Video and Broadcast Specialization at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism in Chicago.

Expected to graduate in September 2025, I bring a unique perspective to storytelling through visual media. My work focuses on community issues, environmental reporting, and human interest stories that matter.

When I'm not behind the camera or working on a story, you can find me exploring Chicago's diverse neighborhoods and cultural scenes, always on the lookout for untold stories and compelling perspectives.
